About the event

Recalibrate is our annual mid-year retreat birth in 2020 during the Covid-19 lockdown, designed to help us pause, reflect, and realign with God’s will individually and collectively. It’s a time to retreat, reboot, re-strategize, and reappraise our lives, ensuring we are walking in step with divine purpose.

This 4-day experience is filled with powerful worship and prayer sessions, deep word-based teachings, spiritual growth activities, and Holy Ghost Services that help us translate our spiritual learnings into faith-based actions while bonding as a community. It’s more than just a retreat; it’s a space to grow, recharge, and move forward with clarity and faith.

God bless you as you join us.

FAQs

What is recalibrate?

Recalibrate Retreat at Hope Nation Church is a spiritual retreat designed to help participants pause, refocus, and realign their lives with God through deep worship, prayer, and biblical teaching. It’s a time of personal and collective transformation, centered on hearing God clearly and growing spiritually.

How many editions have there been ?

3 editions. The first edition was in 2022

Anyone and Everyone
